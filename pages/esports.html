League of Legends

Esports

Esports is the concept where a game is played by professionals and usually streamed to a large audience globally. League of Legends has a lucrative esports scene. The 2021 world finals had a prize pool of 2.18 million U.S. dollars.

While there are many games that currently entertain esports scenes, Riot Games along side their game League of Legends have pioneered the esports we know it today. With the introduction of franchising and collaborations with world class acts, like Imagine Dragons, Riot Games is proving that esports can compete with traditional sports.

2021 Worlds Stats

  • Prize Pool - $2.18 Million
  • Peak Viewers - 4.01 Million
  • Teams - 22
