League of Legends

My Journey

I began playing League of Legends in 2011 and still play to this day. I have met many great friends through this game, and especially during the recent Covid Pandemic is has helped me retain friendships.

I have also partaken in League of Legends esports for the past six years, maintaining a position with a top three team within our local community tournaments. I am currently working on coaching a newer team in order to help grow the local scene.

Esports is the reason I got into web development, since I wanted to be able to create applications and websites that can assist with tournaments, rankings, and other community based activities. I believe esports will play a big role in the future of entertainment, and I feel honoured to be at the beginning of it all.
